Chiken Katsu

Description

Chicken Katsu is a classic Japanese home-cooked meal served with tonkatsu sauce and a side of shredded cabbage salad.

These tender and crispy panko-breaded cutlets are surprisingly easy to make, even on a busy weeknight!

ingredients

  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko
  • Oil for deep fryin
  • Tonkatsu sauce

Step by step guide

  1. Butterfly chicken and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat the chicken with flour, egg, and panko.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown and serve with tonkatsu sauce.
